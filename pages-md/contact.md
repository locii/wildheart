## Finding Us

Wild Heart Psychotherapy is located at **503 Sydney Road, Brunswick**, in the heart of Melbourne's inner north. You'll find us near the corner of Sydney Road and Blyth Street, surrounded by cafes, small shops, and the vibrant energy that makes Brunswick such a welcoming place.

Getting here is easy. If you're coming by tram, the **Route 19 tram stops almost directly outside the building at Stop 22 (Blyth St/Sydney Rd)**. For those travelling by train, it's a short walk from **Anstey Station** on the Upfield line. Several bus routes also run along Blyth Street and nearby Nicholson Street, making public transport a convenient option.

If you're driving, there is usually **1-2 hour street parking available along Sydney Road**, as well as longer-term parking options in nearby residential streets – just be sure to check the signs for time limits.

When you arrive, look for **503 Sydney Road**. You'll find clear signage for Wild Heart Psychotherapy inside. Please aim to arrive at your scheduled time, as waiting areas are limited. If you have any questions about accessibility, parking, or finding the entrance, feel free to get in touch before your session. I look forward to welcoming you here.
