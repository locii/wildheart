# Services

At Wild Heart Psychotherapy, I offer a supportive and practical space to talk through what's happening in your life. People come to therapy for many reasons – feeling stuck or anxious, working through grief or relationship challenges, or wanting to better understand themselves and how they relate to the world.

**Services include:**

- Individual psychotherapy and counselling
- Integration sessions for Holotropic Breathwork or psychedelic experiences
- Somatic and creative therapy approaches
- Process-oriented and Gestalt therapy
- Support for anxiety, depression, grief, and life transitions
- Personal development and self-reflection

### How I Work

My approach is grounded, respectful, and centred around what you need right now. Some sessions may focus on clear goals or specific challenges, while others explore deeper patterns or life questions over time. I draw on different therapeutic frameworks to suit your situation, aiming to make each session a useful step towards greater understanding, relief, or change. If you're considering therapy, you're welcome to get in touch to arrange an initial chat or to book a session to begin this work together.
