## About the Space

The practice room is designed to feel calm, grounded, and welcoming. It features:

- Comfortable seating for individual or couples therapy
- Warm natural lighting
- Climate control for all seasons
- A peaceful atmosphere amidst the lively Brunswick surroundings

## Who This Space Serves

Wild Heart Psychotherapy offers:

- **Individual psychotherapy and counselling** for adults seeking support with anxiety, depression, grief, life transitions, self-exploration, or integration of expanded state experiences.
- **Holotropic Breathwork integration sessions** to support breathwork or psychedelic therapy journeys with grounded psychological and somatic integration.
- **Creative and somatic therapy approaches** drawing on Gestalt, Process-Oriented, and Transpersonal Psychology frameworks.

## Why Brunswick?

Brunswick is a diverse, culturally rich area known for its community-minded feel and accessibility. Being located here makes it easier for people from **Coburg, Carlton, Northcote, Thornbury, Fitzroy, and the Melbourne CBD** to attend sessions without the stress of long commutes.
