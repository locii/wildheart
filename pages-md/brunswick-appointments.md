# Appointments In Brunswick

Sessions are available **in person in Brunswick (Melbourne)** or via **telehealth (Zoom)**. If you are looking for sessions in **Lorne on the Surf Coast**, please visit the [Lorne booking page](https://wildheartpsychotherapy.com.au/appointments/lorne) for availability.

Psychotherapy sessions in Brunswick can be booked for **50 minutes** or **1 hour 20 minutes**. If you're not yet ready to book a full session, you're welcome to schedule a **free 20-minute introductory conversation** to ask questions and explore whether this work feels like a good fit.

If you are interested in **one-to-one breathwork**, please book an initial **50-minute session**. This session is used to orient the work, discuss suitability, and answer any questions. If appropriate, we will then schedule a **three-hour breathwork session** at a later time.
