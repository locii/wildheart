# Lorne Psychotherapy and Counselling

## From February 2026, I will be offering Surf Coast psychotherapy and one-to-one breathwork sessions in Lorne, Victoria.

[BOOK A SESSION](https://wildheartpsychotherapy.com.au/appointments/lorne)

Alongside my Melbourne practice in [Brunswick](https://wildheartpsychotherapy.com.au/finding-psychotherapy-in-brunswick), this expands my work into the surrounds of the Erskine River rainforest, providing a quieter regional setting for depth-oriented psychotherapy and individual breathwork.

I have had a longstanding relationship with Lorne and the Surf Coast, returning regularly for over three decades. I now have the opportunity to spend extended time living and working here, allowing my clinical practice to be shaped more directly by our relationship with place and the natural environment.

Working in a coastal and forested setting offers something distinct from urban practice. Proximity to nature often supports a slower rhythm, greater embodied awareness, and a broader psychological perspective.

Where appropriate, **psychotherapy in Lorne** may also draw on breathwork and nature-based or eco-therapy approaches, supporting a deeper connection to bodily experience and the natural world.

## Location & Practical Info:

Sessions are held 5 minutes from the Lorne town centre on the road to the Erskine River Waterfall. The therapy room has a sweeping view of the Erskine river forest and stretches out towards the ocean towards Point Addis.

Parking is available in the main street of Cora Lynn Crt before turning into the drive. Please [contact me](https://wildheartpsychotherapy.com.au/contact) if you have questions about arrival or scheduling.

[Map: 6/5 Cora Lynn Ct, Lorne VIC 3232]
